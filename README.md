# Power-BI-Project

# Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze and evaluate the performance of a marketing campaign using the dataset provided in **Marketing_Campaign_Data.xlsx** and visualized through **Marketing_Campaign_Dashboard.pbix**.

The goal of this project is to provide a complete analytical overview of campaign effectiveness, customer engagement, revenue impact, and demographic insights—helping stakeholders make informed, data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## **Project Objectives**

* Import, clean, and model raw marketing data
* Build meaningful KPIs for measuring campaign impact
* Visualize customer behavior and demographic segmentation
* Analyze campaign ROI, revenue contribution, and lead conversion efficiency
* Enable drill-down analytics through slicers and interactive visuals
* Provide actionable insights to optimize future marketing campaigns

---

## **Dataset Overview**

The dataset includes customer-level and campaign-level details such as:

* Customer demographics (age, income, marital status, education)
* Campaign interactions (web visits, purchases, responses)
* Spending behavior across product categories
* Campaign acceptance, recency, and engagement metrics

These fields allow for deep segmentation and behavioral analysis.

---

## **Data Processing & Modeling**

### Data Cleaning

* Removed null and inconsistent values
* Standardized data types
* Encoded categorical variables for modeling

### Data Modeling

* Built relationships between customer, campaign, and transaction tables
* Created DAX measures for KPIs and performance indicators
* Ensured a clean star-schema layout for efficient reporting

---

## **Dashboard Features**

The Power BI dashboard includes interactive elements such as slicers, filter panels, and drill-down visuals. Key visuals:

### **KPIs**

* Total Customers
* Total Revenue
* Campaign Response Rate
* Conversion Rate
* Average Customer Spend

### **Visualizations**

* Revenue by Product Category
* Customer Demographic Segmentation
* Campaign Acceptance by Age, Income, and Marital Status
* Spending Patterns and Customer Lifetime Value
* Recency vs. Frequency Behavior
* Web Visits vs. Purchases
* Yearly and Monthly Trend Analysis

---

## **Key Insights**

* Identified high-value customer segments with strong response likelihood
* Determined which age and income groups contributed the highest revenue
* Revealed gaps in customer engagement across digital channels
* Uncovered product categories with the strongest marketing impact
* Highlighted patterns between recency, frequency, and campaign success
